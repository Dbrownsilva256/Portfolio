My Deep Dive

This webpage is showing the crater of Diamond Head in O'ahu, Hawaii. I really just wanted to show the crater itself and some neat surrounding features like the like Diamond Head Lighthouse and Waikiki Beach.

The area Depicted was selected because I am very fond of Hawaii personally and I have visited the crater before so I wanted to map an area that was familiar to me.

The basemap I am using in this map is a roadmap that can show terrain. I think terrain is important to be able to view the landmark of the crater. I decided on a roadmap because I wanted it to be user friendly to know how to get to these places.
